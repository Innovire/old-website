2023 Innovire March Break Camp

Innovire is back with our annual March Break Camp. Next week on Wednesday, Thursday and Friday (March 15th-17th, 2023) between pm and pm ET, we will be hosting workshops and activities on 3D design and printing. At our camp you will have the opportunity to learn how to CAD on OnShape and then put those skills to a test in a car designing competition. You will then have a chance to 3D print your designs and use them to compete against other camp attendees at the 2023 Innovire Summit. Prizes will be awarded!! The camp will be conducted virtually and will be recorded and uploaded online on our YouTube channel too. Wednesday, March 15th to Friday, March 17th from 11 AM to 1 PM ET. If you want an amazing 3 day experience with chances to gain advanced skills in design and 3D printing and loads of fun then make sure to register today!! If you have any questions or concerns feel free to reach out to us and we'd be happy to help!!
