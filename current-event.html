2023 Innovire Sustainability Event

Innovire is back with another event, this time centered around Sustainability!!

We are coming to you in-person with keynotes, workshops and hands-on activities!! You'll have the opportunity to listen to exciting keynotes from incredible industry professionals, learn about how electricity is generated from renewable sources, create solar powered cars, create and see a working miniature electricity generator and get a SNEAK PEAK into what the 2023 Innovire Summit will look like!! The event is completely free, in-person at an incredible venue and provides the opportunity to interact with our wonderful presenters and team!

If you want to celebrate Earth Day by learning about, having fun with and diving deep into the incredibly cool aspects of sustainability and the innovative technologies involved, then the Innovire Sustainability Event is for you. Join us on Saturday, April 29th, 2023 from 12 PM to 3 PM ET at the Corporate Event Centre At CHSI (5110 Creekbank Rd, Mississauga, ON L4W 0A1).
