2023 Innovire Summit

Innovire is thrilled to announce the return of our 5th annual 2023 Innovire Summit!!! Since 2018, our team has attracted up to 350+ youth (between the ages 12-18) from across the GTA to engage in various STEM-related presentations, workshops and competitions. Join us on SATURDAY, MAY 27TH from 9 AM to 4:30 PM ET at The University of Toronto - Mississauga Campus (UTM) (Instructional Centre, 1599 Outer Circle Road, Mississauga, ON L5L 1J7) to be a part of this incredible experience!

WATCH THE TEASER VIDEO HERE:

Our non-profit continuously works to inspire youth to innovate creative solutions to real-world problems and provide high rewarding opportunities to students regardless of their background or life circumstance. In the past, we've had speakers from various companies like Google and MetaVRse, live robotic demonstrations, and hands-on Arduino, bionics & BCI workshops. This year, we would like to introduce our theme "A World Without STEM: What if STEM disappeared from our daily lives?". The theme will focus on how we can be innovators in our own homes and transform ordinary objects to groundbreaking technology. We will have awesome keynote speakers, workshops about emerging technologies, booths with amazing STEM projects and innovations, fun, hands-on team challenges and a FREE PIZZA LUNCH!
