Registration For The 2023 Innovire Summit Now Open!! | Second Episode Of The Innovire Podcast "The Cure To Cancer" Now Up!

Innovire is thrilled to announce the return of our 5th annual 2023 Innovire Summit!!! Since 2018, our team has attracted up to 350+ youth (between the ages 12-18) from across the GTA to engage in various STEM-related presentations, workshops and competitions. Join us on SATURDAY, MAY 27TH from 9 AM to 4:40 PM ET at The University of Toronto - Mississauga Campus (UTM) (Instructional Centre, 1599 Outer Circle Road, Mississauga, ON L5L 1J7) to be a part of this incredible experience! This year, we would like to introduce our theme "A World Without STEM: What if STEM disappeared from our daily lives?". The theme will focus on how we can be innovators in our own homes and transform ordinary objects to groundbreaking technology. We will have awesome keynote speakers, workshops about emerging technologies, booths with amazing STEM projects and innovations and fun, hands-on team challenges!

Learn more and register here: https://innovire.ca/current-event.html.

We are thrilled to say that our second episode of The Innovire Podcast - "The Cure To Cancer" is now up! This episode dives into genetics and finding a cure to cancer. Make sure to give our podcast a listen, find it here: https://spotify.link/KXTJX9g9qyb! Thank you to all of our listeners and we hope you enjoy!

Announcing The Innovire Sustainability Event!!

Innovire is back with another event, this time centered around Sustainability!!

We are coming to you in-person with keynotes, workshops and hands-on activities!! You'll have the opportunity to listen to exciting keynotes from incredible industry professionals, learn about how electricity is generated from renewable sources, create solar powered cars, create and see a working miniature electricity generator and get a SNEAK PEAK into what the 2023 Innovire Summit will look like!! The event is completely free, in-person at an incredible venue and provides the opportunity to interact with our wonderful presenters and team!

If you want to celebrate Earth Day by learning about, having fun with and diving deep into the incredibly cool aspects of sustainability and the innovative technologies involved, then the Innovire Sustainability Event is for you. Join us on Saturday, April 29th, 2023 from PM to PM ET at the Corporate Event Centre At CHSI (5110 Creekbank Rd, Mississauga, ON L4W 0A1). Register today!!
